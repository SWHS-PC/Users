The Haunted House

Mustering your courage, you call out loudly, “Hello! Does someone need help?”

There is no response at first, but then you hear a high, wordless voice somewhere on your left. Aiming your flashlight there, you see a small door, which you had overlooked before because it blends into the dark panelling — a closet perhaps.

Cautiously, you open the door, then laugh as a cat emerges, meowing pitiably. It was only a cat you heard all along! You let the cat outside and continue home, relieved to be spared any further adventure.

THE END
